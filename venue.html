Grand Hotel Sofia

A five-star symbol of classic luxury and hospitality in the heart of the Bulgarian capital.

Grand Hotel Sofia is conveniently located in the very heart of Sofia with exceptional views of the City Garden. It is just steps away from the Ivan Vazov National Theatre, the National Art Gallery, the Alexander Nevski Cathedral, and most of Sofia’s landmarks.

In the immediate vicinity of the Vitosha Boulevard pedestrian zone, you will find numerous shops, unique boutiques, local artisan products, as well as many cafes and restaurants where you can enjoy freshly prepared food and a cozy atmosphere. A place where the urban rhythm meets pleasure.

Getting Here

How to Reach the Hotel

Transport options from Sofia Airport (Vasil Levski) to Grand Hotel Sofia

Private Transfer

A private transfer can be arranged upon request.

  • Distance: 10.2 km
  • Duration: ~17 minutes
  • Travel time depends on traffic conditions

Taxi

Taxis are available at the airport arrivals exit.

  • Taxi stand at the exit of the Arrivals hall
  • Use official taxi stands to ensure fair pricing

Metro (M4)

The most convenient public transport option.

  • Take Line M4 (yellow line) from the airport station next to Terminal 2
  • Exit at Serdika station
  • The hotel is approx. 10 min walk from the station, near the City Garden

Accommodation

Your registration package includes 2 nights at Grand Hotel Sofia with full board (breakfast, lunch, dinner, and coffee breaks).

If you need additional nights, they can be booked for €185 per night.
