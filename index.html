Welcome Message

Welcome to UEMS ORL Section and Board Meeting 2026

Dear delegates of the UEMS – ORL section, dear friends,

Sofia is the heart of Bulgaria – a city where ancient history from Thracian and Roman times meets the modern European pulse. Situated at the foot of Vitosha Mountain, it offers a unique combination of easy access to nature and rich urban culture. With its magnificent temples, cozy cafes, and vibrant rhythm, Sofia is a city that always has something to reveal.

We look forward to welcoming you for a fruitful meeting in a pleasant and friendly atmosphere!

About the Venue

Grand Hotel Sofia is a five-star symbol of classic luxury and hospitality, conveniently located in the very heart of the capital with exceptional views of the City Garden. Just steps away from the Ivan Vazov National Theatre, the National Art Gallery, the Alexander Nevski Cathedral, and most of Sofia's landmarks.

In the immediate vicinity of the Vitosha Boulevard pedestrian zone, lined with numerous shops, unique boutiques, and local artisan products. There you will also find many cafes and restaurants where you can enjoy freshly prepared food and a cozy atmosphere.
